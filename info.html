Safety

Responsible Gambling

Betting should always be fun, never a problem. At AI-77 Betting Lab®, we strongly encourage responsible gambling.

Key principles

  • Never bet more than you can afford to lose
  • Treat bonuses and free bets as tools, not guaranteed profit
  • Take regular breaks and avoid chasing losses
  • Track your bankroll with discipline

Control tools

  • Set deposit, time, and loss limits
  • Use bookmaker tools like self-exclusion
  • Take breaks when needed

Need help?

  • GambleAware
  • GamCare
  • National helplines available in your country

Remember: gambling is for entertainment, not income. Play smart, stay in control, and gamble responsibly.
